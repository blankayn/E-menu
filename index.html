How It Works

Four simple steps from browsing to enjoying your meal. No lines, no waiting, no hassle.

01

Browse the Menu

Scroll through our full menu of drinks, snacks, and meals. Tap any item to see details and customize your order.

02

Add to Cart

Select your items and adjust quantities. The cart updates in real-time with your subtotal and tax.

03

Review & Confirm

Check your order summary. Make any last changes before proceeding to payment.

04

Pay & Enjoy

Tap to pay with card, cash, or mobile wallet. Your order prints instantly to the kitchen.
